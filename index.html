Biography

Yangyang Xu is a seasoned Senior Effects Artist and Technical Director with over a decade of experience creating visually stunning and technically complex effects for feature films, AAA games, and high-end visual effects productions. His work spans large-scale destruction, photoreal fluid simulations, pyrotechnics, and procedural FX system design.

Known for pushing the boundaries of what's possible in real-time and offline rendering pipelines, Yangyang brings both artistic vision and deep technical rigor to every project — from look development through to final compositing integration.
